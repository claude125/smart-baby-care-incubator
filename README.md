## Research Publication

This project is based on the research study:

**Smart Baby Care Incubator with IoT Monitoring**

Published in the *International Journal of Internet of Things and Cyber-Assurance (IJITCA)*.

The research presents an intelligent neonatal care system that integrates **IoT sensors, artificial intelligence, and hybrid power systems** to enhance monitoring and safety for premature and vulnerable infants, particularly in resource-limited healthcare environments.

The system continuously monitors environmental conditions and infant vital signs while providing automated alerts and remote monitoring capabilities for healthcare professionals and caregivers.

---

## System Architecture

The Smart Baby Care Incubator system is designed as an integrated monitoring and analysis platform:


Sensors → Microcontroller → Data Processing → AI Analysis → Monitoring Dashboard → Alert System


### Main Components

- **Environmental Sensors**  
  Monitor temperature, humidity, and air quality inside the incubator.

- **Infant Vital Sign Monitoring**  
  Sensors track body temperature, heart rate, respiratory rate, and oxygen saturation (SpO₂).

- **Microcontroller & Data Processing**  
  Collects sensor data and prepares it for analysis.

- **AI Health Risk Analysis Module**  
  Applies machine learning techniques to detect abnormal conditions and potential health risks.

- **Notification System**  
  Sends alerts via **SMS or email** when abnormal readings or emergencies occur.

- **Remote Monitoring Dashboard**  
  Allows healthcare staff to monitor infant health and incubator conditions in real time.
